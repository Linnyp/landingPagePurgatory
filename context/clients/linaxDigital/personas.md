# **Executive Summary**

This document presents seven research-backed user personas representing the primary customer and prospect segments for Linax Digital’s digital marketing services, SaaS platform, and managed bundle offerings. Each persona is built from the intersection of Linax Digital’s business documentation, current industry research from authoritative sources (LocaliQ, Mordor Intelligence, ServiceTitan, WebFX, Wix/VistaPrint, BDR, HIRI), and Southwest Florida regional market context.

The personas are designed to directly support Linax Digital’s three-lane revenue model by mapping each customer archetype to specific entry points (Lane 1: SaaS, Lane 2: Services, Lane 3: Managed Bundles), identifying their natural migration paths toward higher-value engagements, and quantifying estimated revenue per persona. Together, these personas cover the full spectrum of Linax Digital’s target market: local service businesses from $250K to $10M+ in revenue, 1–50+ employees, across HVAC, plumbing, roofing, remodeling, landscaping, professional practices, lifestyle businesses, and multi-location operators in Southwest Florida.

# **Mike Hernandez**

**"The Scaling Contractor" —** HVAC / Plumbing / Electrical Business Owner

*"I know I need to be online, but every time I try something new it either doesn't work or I can't tell if it's working. I just want the phone to ring with good leads."*

  **DEMOGRAPHICS & FIRMOGRAPHICS**

| Attribute | Detail |
| :---- | :---- |
| **Age** | 38–52 |
| **Gender** | Male (predominantly) |
| **Location** | Cape Coral / Fort Myers metro, Lee County FL |
| **Education** | Trade school or some college; licensed contractor |
| **Household Income** | $120K–$250K personal; business revenue $750K–$3M |
| **Business Size** | 8–25 employees (techs, dispatchers, office staff) |
| **Business Age** | 5–15 years established |
| **Tech Proficiency** | Low-to-moderate; uses smartphone heavily, desktop for QuickBooks |

  **PSYCHOGRAPHIC PROFILE**

Mike is intensely proud of the business he built from his truck. He measures success by truck count and revenue growth. He distrusts marketing agencies because he has been burned before — paying $1,500/month for SEO with nothing to show for it. He respects people who speak in plain language and show measurable results. He is time-poor, spending 10+ hours a day managing crews, handling escalations, and quoting jobs. His wife or office manager currently handles social media sporadically. He is competitive and watches what his rivals are doing on Google, but feels behind.

  **GOALS & MOTIVATIONS**

* Increase inbound call volume by 30–50% without proportionally increasing ad spend

* Dominate the Google Local Pack / Maps for his primary service area

* Build a review engine that consistently generates 5-star reviews

* Systematize lead follow-up so no opportunity falls through the cracks

* Reduce dependency on Angi / HomeAdvisor / shared-lead platforms

  **PAIN POINTS & FRUSTRATIONS**

* Has paid agencies in the past with unclear ROI and no data transparency

* Leads from third-party platforms are expensive and often shared with 3–5 competitors

* No CRM — leads tracked in spreadsheets, sticky notes, or dispatcher memory

* Reviews are stagnant at 60–80 while competitors have 200+

* Website is outdated (built 5+ years ago), not mobile-optimized, slow load times

* No call tracking; cannot attribute which marketing efforts generate revenue

  **BUYER JOURNEY & DISCOVERY**

Mike typically enters via a Google search for marketing help or a referral from another contractor. He responds best to case studies and concrete numbers (“we took a 12-truck HVAC company from 47 to 310 Google reviews in 8 months”). His evaluation process is short — a strategy call, maybe one follow-up. He wants to know the price, the timeline, and what he’ll see in 90 days. He is most likely to enter via Lane 2 (a website rebuild or GBP optimization project) and migrate to Lane 3 (Growth Managed bundle) once he sees results.

  **COMMON OBJECTIONS**

* "I've been burned by agencies before — how are you different?"

* "I don't have time to learn a new platform or deal with another login."

* "Can you just handle everything? I don't want homework."

* "How quickly will I see results? I need leads NOW, not in 6 months."

  **LINAX DIGITAL FIT & REVENUE OPPORTUNITY**

Mike is the ideal Growth Managed ($1,997/mo) or Full-Service Managed ($3,500–$5,000/mo) client. His entry point is typically a website rebuild ($5K–$7.5K one-time) combined with GBP optimization ($500–$1K). The Linax Digital Platform solves his CRM gap, the managed bundle eliminates his time constraints, and the platform transparency directly addresses his past trust issues with agencies. The service-to-platform flywheel is strongest with this persona: every lead from SEO and ads flows into the CRM, building dependency and reducing churn.
