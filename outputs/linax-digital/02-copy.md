## FAQ

- **Section headline:** Questions we get asked a lot.

- **Q1:** How do I know this will actually deliver ROI for my business?
  - A: We start every engagement with an audit so we can show you exactly where you're losing leads before asking you to spend anything. Ongoing services are measured against benchmarks we define together — organic ranking movement, cost per lead, call volume — not vanity metrics. If we're not moving the numbers that matter to your business, we talk about it directly.

- **Q2:** How much time will this take on my end?
  - A: Very little, by design. The discovery call is 30 minutes. After that, most clients spend one to two hours per month reviewing results and giving input on direction. We handle implementation, reporting, and iteration. You don't need to be in the weeds on any of the technical work.

- **Q3:** My business is [HVAC / plumbing / landscaping / charters] — does this actually apply to me?
  - A: Local service businesses are exactly who we built this for. The SEO strategies, lead automation workflows, and ad campaigns we deploy are built around how people in your market search for and choose a service provider — not generic templates repurposed from an ecommerce or SaaS playbook.

- **Q4:** I've worked with agencies before and got burned. Why would this be different?
  - A: The founder writes the code, runs the campaigns, and builds the systems directly — there's no account manager relaying your work to a fulfillment team you'll never speak with. You communicate with the person doing the work. When something isn't performing, you hear about it before we ask you to renew.

- **Q5:** We're busy right now — can this wait until things slow down?
  - A: The businesses that are too busy to fix their marketing are usually busy because of word-of-mouth — which is fragile. When referrals slow down, as they eventually do, there's no digital foundation to catch the gap. The best time to build that foundation is when you have some breathing room. The second-best time is now.
